## 2. Key brand copy and tone

### 2.1 Hero and positioning

- **Hero headline:** “Transforming the future of intelligent marketing”
- **Hero subline:** “Hyperscale digital growth through your ultimate new customer acquisition, UX/CX & MarTech performance agency partner. We are **ONE Agency**.”
- **Section headline:** “The ONE solution for digital advertising performance”
- **CTA section:** “Join ONE Agency in radically transforming the future of intelligent marketing” / “All you need is **ONE**”
- **Footer tagline:** “ONE Agency. Infinite Opportunities.” / “Delivering a Symphony of Digital Performance.”

### 2.2 Value props (homepage)

- **Get big wins, faster** – Outcomes-focused; work backwards from objectives; quick wins + longer-term tactics.
- **Make your mark** – Creative that speaks to the ideal prospect; “creative savants”; ads, messaging, web-to-conversion.
- **Make interactions memorable** – CX journey; thumb-stopping ad → AI support → conversion and beyond.
- **Integrate seamlessly** – No restrictions; integrate with virtually every platform; get live and deliver results.

### 2.3 Product / tech stack

- **myONE Dash™** – Campaign performance, leads, sales, CPL, CPA, CTR, creative performance, impressions; real-time; drill-down to AdSets; live ad creatives desktop/mobile.
- **myONE Dash CRM** – Lifetime journey of every contact; transaction logs, communications (SMS, voice, email), audience targeting, contact details.
- **myONE Dash Automations** – Integration/execution engine; API endpoints, ETL, triggered actions.
- **myONE DataCloud** – 220M+ records, 4.4B+ data fields; 1st/3rd-party; reverse-append, enrich profiles.
- **myONE Go** – URL shortener; tiered, event-based, time-based redirection.
- **myONE Dot** – Cookie-less pixel; navigation/behavioral tracking; works with Facebook/Google pixels.
- **myONE Booking Engine** – Inventory, real-time calendar booking, API-driven, certificate generation.

### 2.4 About / mission

- **Positioning:** “We are a Customer Acquisition Digital Growth Agency.”
- **Mission:** “ONE Agency orchestrates hyper-growth for market dominators. Through a fusion of our proprietary tech stack, persuasive creative and actionable data analytics, we deliver performance-driven digital strategies that propel brands to achieve lasting market leadership.”
- **Story:** Co-founded by John & Marcia Rowley (ICE, ONE) and Jason & Brooke Tremblay (performance marketing, INC 5000).
- **CX:** “Customer experience consistent with customer intent and preferences … right message, right moment, right channel … immersive and conversion-centric creative.”

## 3. Gaps vs current Solvior data

| Area | Reference (oneagency.com) | Current Solvior |
|------|---------------------------|------------------|
| **Solutions** | 8: Customer Acquisition, Performance Creative, Customer Engagement, Referral Engine, Data, Booking Engine, AI Solutions, Accelerated API Integrations | services.json mixes Solutions + Services; missing Customer Engagement, Referral Engine, Booking Engine, AI Solutions, Accelerated API Integrations |
| **Services** | 11 (Paid Search, Paid Social, Programmatic, Marketing Automation, Creative, ORM, Software Dev, Remarketing, CRO, Social Community, Branding) | 8 items in services.json; some mapped as “solutions” in nav |
| **Nav** | Solutions + Services are separate top-level with distinct lists | getNavItems uses one list (from services.json) for both Services and Solutions |
| **Copy** | Full taglines and value props per solution/service (see above) | Partial; can be aligned to reference copy |

---

## 4. Recommendations

1. **Split Solutions vs Services in data**  
   - Add a `type: "solution" | "service"` (or equivalent) and maintain two lists: Solutions (8) and Services (11).  
   - Populate Solutions nav from Solutions list and Services nav from Services list.

2. **Add missing Solutions**  
   - Customer Engagement, Referral Engine, Booking Engine, AI Solutions, Accelerated API Integrations (plus Data as “Data” to match reference).

3. **Add missing Services**  
   - Programmatic, Creative, Online Reputation Management, Conversion Rate Optimization, Social Community Management, Branding & Product Development (and keep Paid Search, Paid Social, Marketing Automation, Software Development & Integration, Remarketing & Retargeting).

4. **Copy and tone**  
   - Use the taglines and value props in Section 2 when writing or updating hero, section headings, service/solution blurbs, and CTAs so tone and message match oneagency.com.

5. **How We Partner / Resources / Company / Support**  
   - Map these sections and URLs in nav and footer so structure mirrors reference where appropriate.
